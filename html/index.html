Rice

type of rice - 種類

cups of rice - カップ

to cook rice - ご飯を炊く

Combine cup(s) of rice with cup(s) of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting.

Cook for about 18 minutes.

for slightly al dente rice - アルデンテご飯

Combine cup(s) of rice with cup(s) of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil.

Bring to a boil and stir once to mix.

Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes.

Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

for softer rice - やわらかいご飯

Increase liquid by 1/2 cup and cook time by 5 minutes.

Begin by inputting your number of cups.
